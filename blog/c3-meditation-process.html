How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

At first I was focusing on getting the main site up, and I kept going my mind deviated from the task at hand and the original goal to the design and colours etc. So originally it was hard but once I was conscious about my mistake I was able to get back onto track.

What did you think about mediation before reading chapter 2?

I always knew that it was an ancient practice carried out by many for the relaxation of the mind, personally though it just wasn’t my piece of cake because sitting for an hour or so didn’t really flatter me.

What new things have you learnt about mediation?

Meditation isn’t only about relaxing one’s mind but also helps to control emotions and associate them with positivity. It’s suitable for anyone and everyone because it gives each individual a chance to do it their own way but making sure the main objective is added.

Did any of the suggested mediation techniques stand out to you?

“Meta-Attention is the secret to concentration” has been applicable to me as mentioned above.

Any other musings?

Mind-body connection… the way meditation helped to not only release stress but also increases immunity levels.
